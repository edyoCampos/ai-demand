Once upon a time, in a land far, far away, there was a very, very small village that was nestled between two very, very large mountains. The people of the village were extremely happy and they lived their lives in a very peaceful and quiet way. They spent their days working in the fields and their evenings telling stories to each other by the fireplace. One day, a very mysterious stranger arrived in the village. This stranger was wearing a very long, black cloak and he had a very mysterious look in his eyes. He gathered all the villagers together and he told them a very, very interesting story about a hidden treasure that was buried somewhere deep within the very, very large mountains. The villagers were all very excited and they all decided to go on a very, very long and dangerous journey to find the hidden treasure. They packed their bags and they set off early the next morning. It was a very, very difficult journey, but they were all very determined to find the hidden treasure and they never gave up. Finally, after many, many days of traveling, they reached the very, very top of the highest mountain and they found the hidden treasure. They were all very, very happy and they returned to their village as very, very rich people.
